About me

Hi! My name is Andrea, I'm 24 years old, and from an early age I was surrounded by technology: my mother developed two flash websites for stamp collectors, my father dabbled in computer hardware assembly when they still looked like old chests.

Growing up among two tech punks like them pushed me to pursue computer science studies... I still remember the joy and excitement after making the first static pages in HTML, or printing out in console the timeless Hello World!

What most intrigues and spurs me on, in Web development as well as in this great universe called programming, is the possibility of creating, realizing, and making available to everyone the unthinkable. I am not there yet, but in my own small way, despite the passing years, I am still surprised by those console messages.
